Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji, also known as Fuji-san or Fujisan in Japanese, is an iconic and sacred stratovolcano located on Honshu, the main island of Japan. It is one of the most recognizable symbols of Japan and a prominent cultural and geographical landmark.











Shibuya Ward

Shibuya is a special ward in the Tokyo Metropolis in Japan. As a major commercial and finance center, it houses two of the busiest railway stations in the world, Shinjuku Station and Shibuya Station.











Tokyo Tower

A wonderful time awaits you at our two viewing platforms, where you can enjoy a panorama of the Tokyo area.











Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle has a rich history dating back to the 16th century. It was originally constructed in 1583 by the warlord Toyotomi Hideyoshi, who unified Japan after a long period of civil wars. The castle played a significant role in Japan's history as a center of power and governance.
